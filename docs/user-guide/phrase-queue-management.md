# Group bars into phrases

[← User Guide](README.md) · Bar Queue

Below the bar queue, a small icon adds a phrase - a song-form section ("Verse", "Chorus") with its own full bar queue. Invisible until you add a second phrase; tap a phrase to jump to it, long-press to remove it.
